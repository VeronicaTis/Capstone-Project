This busniess is called Safe Buddy and it is a sole proprietorship Business. Safe Buddy makes and sells safety weapons for everyone that needs or wants them. The company will be providing safety measures and weapon products for people who need or want them.Every product we are making is a weapon of protection. The comapnies code of ethics are trustworthiness, respect, integrity, accountability, and diversity.

Safe Buddy was created with people's protection in mind. The world is sadly a dangerous place even with government power at hand. Even though the world is dangerous people still have a life to live and multiple things to do. Whether it is just having fun with friends or doing serious business work. No matter what you are doing there will always be danger lurking in the back of your mind. In order to make people feel more protected Safe Buddy has made self protective weapons for people to use; to feel reassured that they at least have a way to protect themselves. It won't stop you from thinking about danger but it will at least help you feel safer when you go out into the world.

MISSION
Our company's mission is to help the world stay safe. The more products and services get bought the more we know that what we are doing is helping.We want to sell over 1million in products and 500k in services in 10 years.

VISION
Our vision is to see that everyone in the world knows about us and knows that there is something around that can keep them safe. Our vision is to be the biggest known company of safety weapons, the go to place when you go to buy your weapons.
